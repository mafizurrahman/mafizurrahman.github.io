About Me

I am a dedicated PhD student in Electrical Engineering at Prairie View A&M University (PVAMU), focusing on the intersection of machine learning and analog computing. My research interests include neural network interpretability through Generalized Additive Models (GAMs) and optimizing deep learning models for neuromorphic hardware implementation.

Currently working at the CREDIT Center under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Lijun Qian, I am developing innovative methods to bridge the gap between high-performance digital neural networks and their efficient analog implementations. My work aims to advance both the theoretical understanding and practical applications of machine learning in resource-constrained environments.
