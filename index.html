About Me

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at Prairie View A&M University, working under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Lijun Qian. My research focuses on Machine Learning, Analog Computing, Deep Learning, and Neurocomputing. I have extensive experience in developing and optimizing deep neural networks (DNNs) for various applications, including image classification, predictive analysis and natural language processing. Besides, I am passionate about developing efficient AI models optimized for neuromorphic hardware. My career goal is to become a Research Scientist, where I can contribute to advancing AI and computational intelligence through innovative research and practical implementations.
